More than 50 thounsands people are affected by the flash flood. We are working to help them by providing them dry food like rice, puffed rice, biscuits, pure drinking water, etc.

Medicine For a Family

Many people are suffering from various diseases like typhoid, cholera, etc. We are working to provide them necessary medicines and primary treatment so that the situation doesn't get worse.

Shelter For a Family

During the flood, many houses are flooded. We are working to provide them shelter so that they can stay safe. We are also working to build and repair their houses as soon as possible.
